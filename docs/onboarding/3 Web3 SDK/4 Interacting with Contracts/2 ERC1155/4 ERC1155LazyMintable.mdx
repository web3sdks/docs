## Batch Lazy Mint NFTs

Batch upload NFT metadata to be lazy-minted to the contract.

You can provide metadata objects to have them uploaded and [pinned](https://blog.web3sdks.com/guides/securing-pinning-your-nft-with-ipfs/)
to IPFS, or provide your own URLs.
