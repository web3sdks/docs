# ERC1155 - Burn NFTs

You can utilize these features of the SDK if your contract implements the [ERC1155Burnable](/contractkit/interfaces/erc1155burnable) interface.

## Burn NFTs

Burn a specified quantity of an NFT.

<Web3sdksFeatureCodeSnippet
  featureName={"ERC1155Burnable"}
  languageFunctionMapping={{
    javascript: "burn",
    react: "useBurnNFT",
  }}
/>

## Burn A Batch Of NFTs

Burn a specified quantity of multiple NFTs at once.

<Web3sdksFeatureCodeSnippet
  featureName={"ERC1155Burnable"}
  languageFunctionMapping={{
    javascript: "burnBatch",
  }}
/>

## Burn NFTs From Another Wallet

Burn a specified quantity of an NFT from another wallet.

<Web3sdksFeatureCodeSnippet
  featureName={"ERC1155Burnable"}
  languageFunctionMapping={{
    javascript: "burnFrom",
  }}
/>

## Burn a batch of NFTs

Burn a specified quantity of multiple NFTs at once from another wallet.
